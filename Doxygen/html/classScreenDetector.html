Detailed Description

The ScreenDetector class Find a screen within an single image.

Finding the projected screen is the first part of the calibration process. The screen is detected by tracking his background color (Generally green).

ScreenDetector determine the coordinates of the four points that represent the four corners of the screen and produce a transformation matrix. Thanks to this transformation matrix we are able to match coordinates taken by the camera with the real coordinates of the computers that is projecting the screen.
